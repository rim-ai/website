Our Mission

Fostering the growth and democratization of AI in Mauritania by promoting AI awareness, encouraging adoption for social good, and facilitating collaboration.

  • Promoting AI Awareness
  • Encouraging Adoption for Social Good
  • Facilitating Collaboration
Our Vision

Being at the forefront of AI education and innovation, shaping a future where AI technology drives progress and creates a positive social impact in Mauritania and beyond.

  • Champion Excellence in AI Education and Research
  • Drive Innovation Through AI Solutions
  • Create a Positive Social Impact
Our Values

The cornerstone of our community, guiding our actions and decisions towards integrity, innovation, diversity, inclusion, collaboration, and social impact.

  • Integrity and Innovation
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Collaboration and Social Impact
